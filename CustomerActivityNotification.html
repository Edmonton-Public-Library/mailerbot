    http://www.epl.ca/myeplonline

Last year cardholders had the opportunity to attend presentations given by Gabor Maté, Roméo Dallaire and George Kourounis to name a few. Learn more at:
    http://www.epl.ca/speakerseries

    http://www.epl.ca/digital-content
